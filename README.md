Privacy Policy – TradeX Journal  


Last updated: 26 January 2026

TradeX Journal respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect user information when you use our mobile application.


Information We Collect
TradeX Journal may collect the following information:

Trade-related data entered by the user (such as asset name, profit/loss, date).


App usage data for improving app performance.


Advertising identifiers for showing ads (via Google AdMob).


We do not collect personal information such as name, email, phone number, or bank details.

Collected information is used to:

Display trade analytics and performance insights.


Improve app stability and user experience.


Show advertisements using Google AdMob.
